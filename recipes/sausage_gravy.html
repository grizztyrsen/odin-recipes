Sausage Gravy

I hope you like sausage gravy!

This recipe will help you to make a savory sausage gravy! This dish goes great with almost any southern style breakfast, but goes especially well with butter biscuits.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pork breakfast sausage
  • 1 tablespoon salted butter or bacon fat
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/3 cup milk
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Brown sausage over medium high heat until no pink reamains. (Do not drain)
  2. Add butter (or bacon fat) and cook to melt. Stir in flour and cook 2 minutes
  3. Add milk a little bit at a time stirring after each addition.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until thick and bubbly
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste

Biscuits

I hope you like biscuits

This recipe will help you to make fluffy biscuits! This dish goes great with almost any southern style breakfast, but it especially compliments sausage gravy.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups self-rising flour
  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Steps

  1. Measure the flour
  2. Add the butter
  3. Cut in the butter
  4. add buttermilk
  5. knead and fold
  6. cut out the biscuits
  7. Bake at 450F for 12-14 minutes
  8. Brush with melted butter as you remove them from the oven.
  9. Wait until butter is absorbed and brush once more
